4 Force Fields

NanoCap implements force fields for the optimisation of the both the dual lattices and carbon lattices. When a structure is saved information relating to the force field is also stored. This allows the same topologies to be optimised by various force fields. Outlined in the next sections are the force fields currently available in NanoCap .
